The Monkees

Who The Monkees Are

The Monkees have been in the music industry since 1966! The group was originally created for a TV show as the American version of the Beatles. Though the group was purposefully created, the band members are all extremely talented musicians. Get to know a little about the members below!

Davy Jones

The Voice of the Band

Davy was the main voice of the band. Born in England, he's the only member who's not from America. Unfortunately, Davy passed away in 2012 after suffering from a severe heart attack. His legacy is carried on by his surviving band mates.

Michael Nesmith

The Rhythm Guitarist

Michael is the song writer and guitarist of the group. His custom guitar and solid song writing are still heard and revered to this day.

Peter Tork

The Man with the Bass

Peter is the man of many talents. While he primarily played the bass, he was able to play multiple instruments and shared a huge passion for recording. Peter is also the oldest of the group, and continues with his music today.

Mickey Dolenz

The Drummer

Mickey actually needed to learned to play thew drums while part of the group. This payed off for him in the end. He is the only member who has been with the band continuously since its the beginning, and the only member with recent recordings of his vocals on all studio albums
